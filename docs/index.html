New in 0.6.0

The largest release the project has shipped. Five storylines pulled together by a single piece of plumbing.

🔔

iOS push notifications, fixed

Settings → Notifications → "Receive motion events from other devices" is now reachable, defaults on after permission, and comes with a Diagnostics screen + a 1,000-record rolling Notification log.

🔎

Cross-day AI search

Type "packages this week" or "vehicles yesterday at the driveway" in All Recordings. On Apple Intelligence devices the prompt runs through an on-device FoundationModels plan; everywhere else, a deterministic regex parser. Recording data never crosses the model boundary.

📅

Visual schedule editors

Two surfaces share a reusable 7×24 weekly grid: one for the camera's recording schedule, one for motion-detection alarms — with optional per-AI-tag overrides ("quiet vehicles overnight, still alert on people").

🔖

Bookmarks that actually download

Background downloads survive relaunches via a launch-time reconciler. Expired-token responses no longer get saved as broken .mp4 files. Every row has an explicit Delete with a confirmation dialog.

🧱

Architecture pass

RecordingsLoader, PollManager, and a three-way CameraStore split (Preferences / Keychain / List) make the recordings tab faster (no stale-data races on rapid date flips), the polling lifecycle testable in isolation, and future surfaces cheaper to build.

✨

UX polish

Battery cameras auto-wake on detail open. iOS / iPadOS restore the last-viewed camera on cold launch. Empty-state messages pin to the top instead of centering in a void. Lazy bookmark enumeration keeps the recordings tab responsive.

Built for the way you watch.

▦

Live multi-camera grids

Adaptive layout fills the screen. Switch to Spotlight, 2×2, 3×3, 4×4, or 5×5 with one tap — on every platform.

↔

Tap-and-hold to rearrange

Long-press any tile and watch them jiggle iOS-home-screen style. Drag to reorder; the order sticks per device.

⌕

Camera search

Type a name to filter the device list — case- and diacritic-insensitive. Find one camera in a 16-channel NVR instantly.

📷

Save Snapshot

Capture the current frame on any tile. macOS writes to your Pictures folder; iOS writes to Photos with add-only permission — Reolens never reads your library.

📅

Recording & motion schedules

Visual 7×24 weekly editors for when each camera records and when motion fires alarms. Per-AI-tag overrides let you quiet a specific tag without silencing the rest.

🔎

Cross-day natural-language search

"Packages this week", "vehicles yesterday at the driveway." On-device FoundationModels handles synonyms and camera-name fuzziness; deterministic fallback everywhere else.

🔖

Bookmarks + background download

Long-press / right-click / swipe to bookmark without playing. The clip downloads in the background — Wi-Fi by default, cellular toggle in Settings — so it's available offline.

▢

Picture-in-Picture (iOS)

Keep a camera in a floating window while you use other apps. Smooth hand-off — no extra decode.

⊕

Pinch-to-zoom (iOS)

Up to 4× digital zoom on the live tile to inspect a license plate or package label. Double-tap to reset.

⟳

Full PTZ control

Pan, tilt, zoom, focus — all 17 PTZ ops from the dedicated control bar, including presets and patrols.

🎙

Shortcuts & Siri

"Hey Siri, open the Front Door camera in Reolens." Plus Show Today's Events and Mute Camera Notifications App Intents. No credentials ever cross the intent boundary.

⚡

Rich alarm notifications

When motion fires, you get a notification with the still that triggered it — not just text. Per-camera mute + per-tag filters + an overnight digest, all syncing across your devices.

📲

Widgets & Live Activities

Camera snapshot, last-motion, and overnight-digest widgets on the Home Screen, Lock Screen, and Control Center. Hub-grouped Live Activities + Dynamic Island for in-flight motion events.

⌘

Native, not a wrapped browser

SwiftUI + Apple's video decoders. Cold launches in under a second, costs almost nothing in battery, and feels like every other Apple app.

↻

Updates itself

Sparkle in-app updates on Mac, TestFlight on iOS. We never phone home with anything else.

☁︎

Mac, iPad, iPhone — synced

Camera list, grid layouts, rotations, codec preferences sync via iCloud Drive. Passwords stay per-device in Keychain — never copied off the device they were entered on.

🔓

Open source

Swift 6, MIT-licensed, on GitHub. Engineering principles in AGENTS.md, security policy in SECURITY.md.

System requirements

  • Mac — macOS 26 Tahoe or later, Apple Silicon or Intel
  • iPad / iPhone — iPadOS / iOS 26 or later
  • Reolink cameras, NVRs, or Home Hubs reachable on the local network
  • HTTP/HTTPS access to each device's CGI port (default 80 / 443)
  • iCloud account (optional) — used to sync the camera list and relay motion events between your own Apple devices

Users on macOS 14 / iOS 18 should stay on the 0.4.x security-backport track. FoundationModels-driven features (Today digest, NL search) fall back to deterministic implementations on devices without Apple Intelligence.
